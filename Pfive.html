Online Book Store

A platform where users can buy and sell books with just one click. A user-friendly Online Bookstore project in which users can log in or register, view available books, select books along with their quantity, and buy them. The project can also be used by the administrator, who can add new books and remove books. To sell books, clients have to contact the administrator using the message service.
